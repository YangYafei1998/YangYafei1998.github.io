Unsupervised Object Segmentation for Real-world Image
Yafei YANG, Bo YANG

Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2022
webpage

We systematically investigate the effectiveness of existing unsupervised models on challenging real-world images. We proposed four complexity factors and corresponding ablations to quantitative evaluate the objectness bias within datasets and models.
