Hi, there! This is Yafei YANG. Welcome to my web-page.

I am a PHD student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I am supervised by [Dr.YANG Bo](https://yang7879.github.io/) at [vLAR](https://vlar-group.github.io/) group. My research interest is on accurate and efficient recognition, segmentation and reconstruction of all individual objects within real-life scenes.
Before that, I completed my B.Eng degree at University of Hong Kong where I have participants research projects related to unsupervised object detection in assembly lines under the supervision of [Prof.Wenping Wang](https://www.cs.hku.hk/people/academic-staff/wenping).
